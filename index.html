Fondly,
Laura Morello Harris

FAQ
How do I RSVP?
Fill out the form above or contact Laura Morello Harris at Lmharris8@gmail.com or 205-915-8676
What is the deadline to RSVP?
Please RSVP for the luncheon and meet 'n' greet to Laura by Sunday, October 2, 2022.

Please also have any accommodations and activities booked through the resort by that time as well.
What is the schedule of events?
We are offering a complimentary meet 'n greet on Friday for those who are able to stay the entire weekend. Come early and catch up with friends and family!

Saturday is a free day for exploring the resort, enjoying time with friends and family, and relaxing. See the activities section for more specifics on Saturday activities.

We are officially celebrating Charles and Bonnie's 50th wedding anniversary during the luncheon on Sunday.
Do I need to spend the night at the resort?
No. You can choose to drive to the resort just for the luncheon and return home afterward.
If I want to spend the night, what are the accommodations?
If you opt to stay at the resort, you are responsible for booking your own accommodations. There are multiple options at the resort.
  • There are cottages, which can have one bedroom or multiple bedrooms. You can book a one bedroom side of a duplex cottage for one family, or you can book an entire cottage with multiple rooms for a large family or multiple families.
  • You can also book a room at the inn, which is more of a hotel-style setting.
You can call the resort at 877.773.2447 to make whichever reservation you prefer and ask questions. Please reference the "Morello Wedding Anniversary" event for our special rate.
What amenities are provided in the rooms?
All rooms are furnished with: a mini fridge, keurig coffee maker, bar/prep sink, soap / shampoo / conditioner / body wash, towels, robes, a hair dryer, safe, and ironing board.

The cottages also have a living room, fireplace, small eating area, porch, and most have a swing as well.

Daily maid service is provided for the cottages upon request only; you can dial 0 on your room phone to request the service.
What amenities are provided at the resort?
Complimentary wifi is provided throughout the resort, and free parking is available everywhere.

Guests can use the pool, fitness cener, and hiking/biking trails. They can tour the property - manor house ruins, gardens, museum, and family cemetery.

Complimentary catch-and-release fishing, access to canoes for the lake, access to bicycles, lawn games at the green, petting barn animals, and firepits with s'mores are all available to guests.

Guests can also call the front desk to have an attendant pick them up in a golf cart and take them to their destination.
What other activities are available?
You are responsible for booking any activities you would like to participate in during your stay through the resort. We encourage you to get a group together for an activity!

For a fee you can book several activities with advanced notice. You can find the official list here.

These include: spa services, horseback riding, beginner corral rides, introduction to archery / axe throwing / air rifles, and disc golf.

You can also play a round of golf, or sign up for sporting clays or wing shooting.
What dining options are available at the resort?
You have four options for food at the resort (click to find out more about them, along with their days and hours of operation):
  • Beer Garden: This is the most casual dining option and is outside only (hours may vary due to weather). It is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner and serves your favorite beer and wine selections.
  • Woodlands Grill: This is a flexible dining option, with options from burgers to filets. The attire is resort casual; t-shirts and jeans are fine, but you may feel more comfortable in slightly nicer attire.
  • Woodlands To Go: You can order food delivered to your room or cottage from the Woodlands Grill to go menu.
  • Rice House: This is the most formal option at the resort; they request collared shirts aka dressy resort casual. Typically they serve a 3 or 4 course meal with limited options - very good!
Note: Reservations are required for any meal at the Woodlands Grill and Rice House, but you can make them the day-of if needed.
What should I pack to bring?
Unless it rains, both the meet 'n' greet and the Sunday lunch will be outside. Typical weather for this time of the year at the resort is between 50-70 degrees.

Consider wearing shoes that are appropriate for grassy areas or uneven surfaces. A light jacket and layers are encouraged.

A mixture of casual day-wear and nicer evening attire is encouraged; please pack according to the activities you plan on attending.
Who can I contact at the resort?
You may call the main Barnsley number (877-773-2447) to book accommodations, dining reservations, and activities prior to your stay. This is strongly encouraged.

While at the resort, you can use the phone in your room to also contact staff members:
  • 0 - Reception & Daily Maid Services
  • 2500 - Dining Reservations
  • 2300 - Spa Reservations
  • 2800 - Outpost (Booking Activities)
For general help, feel free to approach the front desk at the inn. Also remember the Outpost carries apparel, snacks, and other miscellaneous items you forgot to pack!
Are children welcome?
Yes! Children are welcome and encouraged at the resort. There are many activities for them to enjoy. We just ask that you be responsible for your own children for the weekend if you choose to bring them.

The meet 'n' greet is open to all ages. However, the Sunday luncheon be reserved for adults 13+, and we are providing childcare for the luncheon. Please let us know if you will need this service when you RSVP.
Should we bring gifts?
No gifts, please. After 50 years of marriage, Charles and Bonnie have all the material items they need and would love your presence most of all.
